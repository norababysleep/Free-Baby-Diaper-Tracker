Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 10, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: At Nora Baby Sleep, we respect your privacy. Our parenting tools are built to work locally in your browser—meaning we don’t collect or store your baby’s data on our servers. We only use anonymous analytics to understand how our tools are used so we can improve them.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Nora Baby Sleep (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles your information when you use our free web-based parenting tools, such as our baby trackers, calculators, and planners.

2. Data We Collect

We design all tools to function without requiring personal accounts or cloud storage. The only data we may collect is minimal and anonymous, used solely to improve performance and usability.

  • Local Data: Data you enter (e.g., baby sleep logs, feeding times) is stored in your browser’s local storage—only you can access it.
  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to track anonymized usage (page views, clicks, and time spent) to improve site performance. This does not include personal information.
  • Contact Data: If you email us directly, your email address and message are used only for correspondence.

3. No Account or Login Required

All tools are free to use—no sign-up, login, or personal data submission is required. You can use, export, or delete your data anytime directly from your browser.

4. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies to:

  • Understand how visitors use our tools
  • Detect and fix performance issues
  • Remember dark/light theme preferences

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Our site still works without them, although some preferences (like theme settings) may reset.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • All baby-related input data is saved locally on your device.
  • We do not store or transfer baby logs, names, or personal information to our servers.
  • Analytics data is stored by Google Analytics according to their privacy terms.

6. Sharing of Information

We never sell, trade, or rent user data. We may share anonymized analytics data with trusted partners to help improve the site’s performance—but this data cannot identify you or your baby.

7. Third-Party Services

Our website may include links to external resources, such as our blog, shop, or affiliate links. These sites have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices or content.

8. Children’s Privacy

  • Our tools are designed for parents and caregivers—not children under 13.
  • We do not knowingly collect any personal information from minors.
  • If we become aware that information was submitted by a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

9. Your Data Rights

Because our tools run locally, you are in complete control of your data. You can:

  • Delete your saved data anytime by clearing your browser’s local storage.
  • Export or back up data from tools that offer download options.
  • Contact us if you have privacy concerns or need help removing stored data.

10. Advertising and Affiliates

Some pages may display third-party ads or affiliate links. Clicking them may redirect you to external sites that use cookies or tracking. We do not control those cookies and recommend reviewing the third-party’s privacy policies.

11. Security

We use industry-standard HTTPS encryption and rely on secure hosting services. However, as no system is 100% secure, you should take precautions when sharing personal information online.

12. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect new features, legal changes, or improvements. Updates will be posted here with a new “Last Updated” date. Continued use of our tools means you accept the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests about privacy, reach out to us:

  • Email: norababysleep@gmail.com
  • Website: https://norababysleep.store

We Respect Your Trust

Your baby’s data belongs to you. Our mission is to make safe, transparent, and privacy-friendly parenting tools that empower—not exploit—families.
