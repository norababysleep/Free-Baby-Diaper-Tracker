Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days (Monday-Friday). For urgent technical issues affecting many users, we prioritize those first.

Do you offer phone support?

At this time, we provide support exclusively via email at norababysleep@gmail.com. This allows us to keep our tools free while providing quality support to all users.

Can you help with medical questions about my baby?

We cannot provide medical advice. Our tools are designed to help you track patterns, but any health concerns should be discussed with your pediatrician. We can help you export your tracking data to share with your doctor!

I'm having trouble with a tool. What should I include in my email?

Please include: (1) which tool you're using, (2) what browser and device you're on, (3) what you were trying to do, (4) what happened instead, and (5) any error messages or screenshots. This helps us troubleshoot faster!

Can I suggest a new tool or feature?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Email us at norababysleep@gmail.com with your ideas. While we can't implement every suggestion, we carefully consider all feedback when planning new features.

Are you hiring or accepting contributors?

We occasionally look for guest bloggers, content creators, and collaborators. If you're interested in contributing, please email us with details about your background and what you'd like to contribute.

How do I report a security concern?

If you've discovered a security vulnerability, please email us immediately at norababysleep@gmail.com with the subject line "Security Issue". We take security seriously and will respond promptly.

Can I share my success story?

We'd love to hear it! Email us your story about how our tools have helped your parenting journey. With your permission, we may feature it on our website to inspire other parents.
